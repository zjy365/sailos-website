Cross-cloud regulatory compliance refers to ensuring adherence to data protection regulations such as GDPR or HIPAA in multi-cloud architectures. Its importance lies in avoiding legal risks and safeguarding data security, and it is applicable to sensitive industries like finance and healthcare.

The core components include unified policy management, tool integration such as Kubernetes' OPA (Open Policy Agent), and automated monitoring and auditing. This achieves consistent compliance through cloud-native platforms, impacting areas such as enhanced data governance and reduced violation incidents.

Processing steps: Define compliance requirements; deploy cross-cloud monitoring tools; automate policy enforcement; conduct regular audits. Business values include reducing the risk of fines, enhancing enterprise trust, and supporting global deployment.
